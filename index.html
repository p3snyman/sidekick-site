How it works

From @mention to answer in seconds

Six steps, fully automated. You just ask.

1

You @mention the bot

In any authorized Slack channel. Attach files, paste code, ask anything.

2

Auth check

Bot verifies the user's email against the allowlist. Unauthorized users get a polite rejection.

3

Message classified

LLM classifier (with keyword fallback) routes to the right tier: quick question or real task.

4

Context enrichment

Optional hooks inject relevant context — Jira tickets, doc search results, data dictionaries.

5

LLM executes with tools

Claude or Gemini CLI called with the right model, tool restrictions, and skills. Live progress in thread.

6

Response posted

Answer in the thread with a cost footer showing tokens and price. Done.
